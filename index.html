Eternal Conflict

  • High Concept: The forces of light of and dark are locked in an eternal conflict in a game of tag meets dodgeball.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should enjoy the fast-paced gameplay and feel competitive as they strive to be victorious.
  • Genre: This game is a competitive action game.
  • Game play: Players move with the arrow keys and WASD keys. The goal is to either avoid the moving orb, or to tag the other player by hitting them from above. This can be accomplished by holding the down key while the opponet is beneath you. Until a winner is determined the orb will slowly increase its movement speed.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype evaluates aerial gameplay.
  • Features: Aerial Movement: Gameplay include complete eight directional movement. Fast Paced Gameplay: Difficultly increases the longer the match lasts. Two Ways to Win: Be the last man standing or go on the offense.
  • Setting/Premise: The forces of light and dark have been locked in an eternal conflict since the beginning of time. A powerful orb has been realeased onto thier battleground to end the conflict. The forces continue to fight to the last man standing.
  • Story: The forces of light and dark have been locked in an eternal conflict since the beginning of time. A powerful orb has been realeased onto thier battleground to end the conflict. The forces continue to fight to the last man standing.
  • Target Audience: This game is recommended for ages 10+.
  • Play time: Games typically last 1 to 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: The player who is higher on the screen always has the advantage. While being at the very top makes it so only the orb can defeat you, you are limited to dodging to the left and right.
  • Prototype Assets: Prototype assets made from scratch. Sounds from soundbible.com.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The game mostly closely resembles dodgeball, with the difference that the ball remains in motion rather than being thrown.
